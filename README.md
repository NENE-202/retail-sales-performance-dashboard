## â“ Business Problem This Project Solves
The business faced three key challenges:
- Lack of clarity on **revenue performance over time**
- Difficulty identifying **high-performing products and categories**
- Limited understanding of **regional sales performance and gaps**

Without these insights, it becomes difficult to:
- Allocate inventory effectively  
- Focus marketing efforts  
- Improve underperforming regions  

This dashboard solves these problems by providing a **clear, visual summary of sales performance** across time, products, and regions.

---

## ğŸ“Š Key Insights from the Analysis

### Revenue Performance
- Revenue increased significantly from **$1,950 in September** to **$7,680 in October**, indicating strong short-term business growth.

### Product Performance
- **Electronics and Furniture** were the strongest categories.
- **Furniture led in units sold (44 units)**.
- Products such as **Monitors, Printers, and Desks** generated higher profits, showing stronger demand and better returns.

### Regional Performance
- **West region:** Highest revenue ($2,780)
- **North region:** $2,600
- **South region:** $2,410
- **East region:** Lowest performance, indicating potential pricing, demand, or market awareness challenges.

### Key KPIs
- **Total Revenue:** $9,630  
- **Total Profit:** $2,631  
- **Total Units Sold:** 71  
- **Revenue per Order:** $642

## ğŸ¯ How These Insights Support Business Decisions

- **Inventory & Supply Decisions:**  
  Focus on stocking high-performing products such as Monitors, Printers, and Furniture to maximize revenue and profit.
